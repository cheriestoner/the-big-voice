The Big Voice

THIS RESEARCH PROJECT aims to explore the combination of speech synthesis and interactive music production, and to inherit the close connection between technology and music. Also, this research will provide a solution covering applications and social platforms from the all-round perspective of music production, distribution and reception. The goal of this research is to build a real-time interactive creation of a voice mixing application that is based on user input and a cloud database, and provides a platform for user research and interaction.
TEAM MEMBERS: Xuehua Fu, Zhaorui Liu, Jiayi Wen, Marcel Sagesser, Sylvia Lee
